About Us

About Us

Roaming Phlebotomy and Wellness Center (RP&WC) is a Kenyan Center for Phlebotomy services and provision of Wellness health checks for individuals, Corporates and organized groups. RP&WC mainly outsources phlebotomy services to medical and research institutions. This is through a well-trained strong, committed team, dedicated to providing the highest standard of blood collecfion.

Our mission

a) Minimize patients’ hassles and cost to access laboratory services. We take the responsibility of traffic jams and hospital cues to ensure results are timely delivered.

b) Connect patients to service providers at a time and locafion of their desire, convenience and choosing.

c) Connect patients to high quality and affordable medical service providers

Our vision

Minimize patients’ struggle and trouble in seeking medical services that they can otherwise receive at their convenience and comfort.

Delivering health services at doorsteps

Our responsibilities

a) To collect and deliver quality specimens for processing as may be requested by patients or their medical practitioners.

b) Responsible for the safety and suitability of specimens in our custody.

c) To timely deliver results or any information about our clients’ tests.

d) To source service providers with quality services at affordable costs.

e) To maintain a highly qualified team; with the right training, qualifications and experience.

f) To maintain patient confidentiality at all times.

RESULTS HANDLING AND DELIVERY

Patients’ results will be handled with utmost confidentiality. Once ready, results will be shared as per the patients’ instructions. RP&WC will share results by email or hardcopies directly to the client, a declared next of kin and/or a declared medical practitioner/s.
