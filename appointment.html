HOW TO BOOK FOR MOBILE SAMPLE COLLECTION:

Book Appointment

a) Call our customer service number +254 700 693 956. or email us on info@rpwc.co.ke for booking.

b) Patents with doctors’ requisitions may be asked to share the request forms on email or WhatsApp messenger.

c) You will be required to fill-in our application form giving instruction on the locafion and the desired time for sample collection.

d) You will also fill-in the facility you would like your sample processed.

e) Payments for the test will be done directly to the facility you want your sample processed.
Proof of payment must be shared before samples are collected. Patients with billing arrangements will be required to have pre authorizations beforehand

f) Payment for mobile sample collection services to be paid via RP&WC official MPESA Till number.

g) Receipts to be shared immediately payments are completed.

RESULTS HANDLING AND DELIVERY

Patients’ results will be handled with utmost confidentiality. Once ready, results will be shared as per the patients’ instructions. RP&WC will share results by email or hardcopies directly to the client, a declared next of kin and/or a declared medical practitioner/s.
