Support Services

RP&WC therefore developed a range of services to support and address the needs/gaps. These services include mobile specimen collections for laboratory testing and performing basic health procedures such as Blood Pressure checks, body weights and heart rates.

It is through the mobile phlebotomy and Wellness that patient have continued to receive the following benefits:

a) Save Time and Increase Safety and Accessibility.

Saves patients’ time driving to medical facilities for sample collections. This in turn, patients avoid long queues at the medical facilities, create personalized experience and accessibility of services for those with mobility challenges. It also minimizes Turn-around-time for results.

b) Build Relationships.

Mobile services make patients feel more at ease when some procedures are performed in their comfort. It also creates rapport, confidence and assurance for patients who require frequent blood draws

c) Increase Privacy.

Mobile phlebotomy services increase privacy for patients especially for those who may desire to avoid visits to labs due to crowding and other personal concerns.

d) Cost-Effective and Time-Saving.

Healthcare visits at homes offer a cost-effective alternative by reducing transport cost and time.
